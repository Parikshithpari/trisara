Trisara sits in Byatarayanapura on Bellary Road — close enough to Hebbal and Kempegowda International Airport that out-of-town family can land and arrive within the hour, and close enough to Esteem Mall and Phoenix Mall of Asia that the rest of your guest list has no excuse either.

Inside, a contemporary banquet hall holds the formal parts of the day. Outside, an open lawn takes over once the sun drops — string lights, open sky, and enough room to let a celebration breathe. Every booking comes with an in-house team for décor, lighting and catering, so the day is planned once, with people who already know the space.
