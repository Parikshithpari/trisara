The Hall

A climate-controlled, chandelier-lit banquet hall for the formal core of your event — stage ceremonies, seated dinners, and receptions that run late into the night without weather ever entering the conversation.

The Lawn

Trisara's own take on the open-air trend — a lawn dressed in string lights once evening falls, built for mandaps, cocktail hours, and the part of the night nobody wants to end early.

  • Open lawn suited to mandap and stage setups
  • Evening lighting designed in-house
